Community Service Opportunity

King is hosting Fast Camp for incoming 9th graders. This is a great opportunity to earn Community Service hours!

About four to six FBLA members are needed to help run the event and promote FBLA.

Volunteers are needed from Tuesday, July 27th to Thursday, July 29th from 8:15AM to 12:45PM each day.
Training is on Monday, July 26th. The time will be sent out to interested members.

If you are interested and need Community Service hours, please email Ms. Mosley at deborah.mosley@sdhc.k12.fl.us with your name, student number, and your grade level.
